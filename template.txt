## FORMAT ##

{Start Time} - {End Time} {Optional NB or NC}
{Ticket Number} {Client} - {Description of Ticket}
{Description of time entry, multiline}


## EXAMPLE ##

9a - 9:15a NB
376526 Client One - General Tasks
-DSU

9:15a - 9:30a
1048190 Client Two - Special Project
-Did some work
-Did some other work
